About The Vacuum Wizard

The Vacuum Wizard are independent specialists in Dyson handheld vacuum cleaners & spare parts, Based between Bristol & Bath, this website list most if not all Dyson Spares required for the following handheld models. Dyson V6, Dyson V7, Dyson V8, Dyson V10 and the latest Dyson V11. If you are unsure of the part you need or cannot find it on our website, please feel free to Contact us here with your query and we will be happy to look into your request. We currently only stock and specialise in Dyson handhelds.

The Vacuum Wizard also offer a repair and servicing service for our local customers. Most repairs and services available while you wait as we have large amounts of parts in stock.

Please always contact us prior to travelling to confirm service availability.
